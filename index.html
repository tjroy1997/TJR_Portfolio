About Me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the University of Houston specializing in Measurement, Quantitative Methods, and Learning Sciences. My research areas include digital health education, health informatics, motivation, grit, engagement, academic procrastination, and self-efficacy. With a strong foundation in computer science and engineering, I integrate advanced statistical methods with machine learning to address real-world problems in education and healthcare.
